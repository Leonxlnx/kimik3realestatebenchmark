Halcyon Estates presents

Stillwater House

A cantilevered villa of charred timber and glass above a still mountain lake — 1,240 metres up, where the water keeps the sky.

The house, and who we are

Halcyon Estates is a private brokerage and design house for rare properties. Since 1998 we have placed fewer than four hundred homes — each one chosen the way this valley chooses its weather: slowly, and without apology.

Stillwater House is our fourteenth commission on this shore: five pavilions of local stone, blackened cedar and low-iron glass, held above the waterline on a single concrete spine.

What we do

  • Private Brokerage — discreet sale and acquisition of significant homes.
  • Architectural Curation — we commission, restore and finish houses worth keeping.
  • Off-Market Access — the majority of our placements are never listed.
  • Stewardship — year-round care of the estates we place.

Selected residences

  • 01 · Glasshouse — Lake Como. 620 m² of low-iron glass and travertine above the water. Guide on request.
  • 02 · Cedar Court — Kyoto. 480 m² around a walled courtyard of maple and moss. Guide on request.
  • 03 · The Long Barn — Cotswolds. 710 m², a restored agricultural hall with a twenty-metre gallery. Guide on request.

How we work

  1. Listen — the site, the light, the life.
  2. Compose — architecture as a sequence of views.
  3. Build — craft verified at every joint.
  4. Care — stewardship beyond the sale.
